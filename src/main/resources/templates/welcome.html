• About

Founded in 1998, in North West Baltimore County, Maryland, NovairGroup, Llc. is a minority woman-owned small business focused on providing software systems development services to the Federal Government.

Our team has delivered data systems and business intelligence and analytics programs for well-recognized brands and global enterprises. Our track record and mission are clear: we commit to working closely with our clients to enable their success. We become trusted advisors.

As a premier data services provider, NovairGroup manages the complexities of data and data driven systems through our Secure Managed Cloud Services. You can trust your analytics will be delivered with excellence by our data science and software engineer experts every time helping business and marketing leaders make smart and informed business decisions quickly.
